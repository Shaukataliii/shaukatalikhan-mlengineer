Cources and Certifications

These are the courses I completed on my journey to becoming a Machine Learning Engineer.

  • AI Basic Course

    This was my first course in Artificial Intelligence, taught by the esteemed Sir Irfan Malik (CEO of Xeven Solutions) and his team. It provided me with a foundational understanding of AI concepts.

  • Python for Data Science

    Although I was already familiar with Python, this course introduced me to advanced concepts like map and lambda functions, as well as essential libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib.

  • Statistics

    As I progressed, I realized the importance of statistics. Having previously studied many of the core concepts during my B.Com, I found this course to be a smooth continuation of my learning.

  • Machine Learning

    Diving deeper into the field, I explored supervised and semi-supervised learning algorithms in this course by Krish Naik (a Data Scientist at INeuron and an excellent educator).

  • Deep Learning

    After gaining a solid understanding of ML algorithms and some practical experience, I decided to delve into Deep Learning with a course by Sir Nitish at CampusX (YouTube). Compared to ML, DL presented greater challenges in design and computational requirements, which I was still working to overcome at that time.

  • Natural Language Processing

    I continued my learning journey with an NLP course by Sir Nitish at CampusX (YouTube). As a beginner and aspiring practitioner, I was eager to grasp the mechanics behind NLP and found this course even more engaging than Deep Learning.
